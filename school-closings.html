When Are School Closings Announced?

Most Tennessee school districts announce closings between 5:00 AM and 6:30 AM on the day of closure. For major anticipated weather events like winter storms, many districts announce the evening before. We recommend signing up for our email alerts to receive notifications as soon as closings are announced.
